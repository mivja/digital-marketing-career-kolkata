Common questions — digital marketing & SEO in Kolkata

Straightforward answers, no fluff. based on conversations with local professionals.

Do I need coding for SEO?

Basic HTML/CSS understanding helps, but not mandatory. Focus on content, structure, and tools.

Which SEO tools are used most?

Google Search Console, Ahrefs, SEMrush, and local rank trackers. Many start with free versions.

Is local SEO different for Kolkata?

Yes — optimizing for "near me" in Bengali + English, local citations from city directories matter.

How long to see SEO results?

Typically, 4–6 months for organic traction, but local packs may show earlier movements.

Salary expectations for SEO executive?

Entry-level ranges 18k–28k/month, with hikes as you handle campaigns independently.

Is freelancing possible from Kolkata?

Absolutely — many work with Delhi/Mumbai clients or global platforms. Payment via Razorpay/PayPal.

Do agencies train freshers?

Yes, many agencies in Kolkata have 3-month internship tracks with stipends.

What about performance marketing?

Google Ads & Meta Blueprint certifications add weight, but practical campaign experience wins.

Is content writing a separate role?

Often combined with social media or SEO. Bilingual (Bengali+English) is an edge.

Best way to build portfolio?

Run a small blog, help a local store with their Google listing, document results.

Do startups hire digital marketers?

Yes — they need growth hackers; generalist with SEO+Ads+social is preferred.

Email marketing demand in Kolkata?

Growing in e‑commerce and edtech; tools like Mailchimp are common.

What is 'local citation'?

Mentions of business name, address, phone on directories — important for local SEO.

Can I work remotely for outstation firms?

Yes — many Kolkata marketers work for Mumbai/Delhi agencies fully remotely.

Any specific sectors hiring?

Education, tourism, healthcare, and food delivery apps in Kolkata.

Difference between on-page & off-page?

On-page: content/HTML changes. Off-page: backlinks, social signals, citations.

Is there demand for video editors?

Yes — short‑form content for reels/youtube is booming alongside marketing.

What is CTR in SEO?

Click‑through rate from search results — influenced by title tags and meta descriptions.

Do I need to learn Python or R?

Not at the start. Excel and Google sheets are enough for most marketing roles.

How to find a mentor in Kolkata?

Attend local meetups (Salt Lake, New Town), LinkedIn outreach works too.

Is blogging dead for SEO?

No — useful long‑tail content still drives organic traffic, especially for local queries.

What is EEAT?

Google's concept: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness — content quality matters.

Which ad platform is best for local?

Google Local Service Ads followed by Meta for awareness.

Are there government digital programs?

Yes, NSDC and skill development courses sometimes tie up with training partners.

Should I specialise early?

Generalist first (1 year), then pick SEO/Ads/content based on interest.

How to measure SEO success?

Organic traffic, keyword rankings, leads from contact forms or calls.

Do companies value Google certifications?

They help get interviews, but practical test tasks are common.

Can I learn while working part‑time?

Yes — many evening courses and online modules exist.

What's the future of SEO in 2026?

AI overviews & search generative experience — but local intent stays strong.

Is digital marketing stressful?

Like any client‑facing role, deadlines and data fluctuations exist, but are manageable.
